Research Mission

Our research focuses on developing, assessing, and implementing patient-centered AI solutions that ease healthcare delivery and improve patient outcomes. Using expertise in NLP, machine learning, and biostatistics, we analyze the full patient journey to uncover insights from patient–doctor communication while contributing to AI governance and ethical policy development in healthcare.

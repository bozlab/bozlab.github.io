## About

The lab focuses on study design, measurement, and inference for real-world data. We develop methods for extracting and validating signals from unstructured and patient-generated data, and for evaluating AI systems using real-world evidence.

## Research

### Real-world evidence
Methods for generating valid, reproducible evidence from routine health data.

### Unstructured data
NLP and LLM approaches to extract measurable constructs from clinical text and patient-reported data.

### Evaluation of AI
Use of real-world data to assess performance, generalizability, and limitations of AI systems.

### Responsible methods
Transparency, reporting standards, bias assessment, and measurement validity.
